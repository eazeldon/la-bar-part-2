About

La Bar and La Bar avenue

Was created by three friends wanting a trendy and cozy restaurant and lounge bar where they can bring there families, children and friends. La Bar opened on June 30, 2017, La Bar avenue opened on March 8, 2020 and become a famous place. All three of them have a very successful past in their chosen fields.

Marie-owner

Marie worked as an assistant to successful interior decorators before setting up her own business and making several award winning designs. Marie has made all the interior design work for La Bar and La Bar avenue.

James-bartender

James is a former olympic beach volley ball player and won an Olympic bronze medal. After his sport career James worked in an award winning cocktail bar in Manhattan.
